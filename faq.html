FAQ'S


The booth is very easy to use! Just get in, smile, and press the big button. The booth will take 3 shots approximately 5-10 seconds apart; each photo will be displayed on a monitor inside the booth. Seconds later the pictures are printed and available outside the booth. 

Is the booth easy to use? Can the photo strips be personalized?
Yes, we can add a personalized logo to the bottom of each strip. Please inquire for more details. 

Aren’t all photo booths kind of the same?
Here’s the deal: NOT all photo booths are created equally. If you care about what your photo booth looks like and how it performs, then you are right where you should be. And we’re glad to have you! 

It seems like your prices are a little higher than some other companies. Why? 
​The old adage “you get what you pay for” is so very true. If you pick your event vendors simply based on price then you might want to go to another company. But, if you are looking for top-of-the-line quality and something more than a homemade rod-and-curtain stall, you know, something that actually looks good at your event, then you’ll like what we have to offer.

Is there a limit on how many photos we can print at the event?
No. Each package comes with unlimited prints during your event!
